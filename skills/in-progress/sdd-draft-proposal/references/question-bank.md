# Proposal Question Bank

Use these as language-neutral intents. Ask one at a time and attach a recommended answer.

1. What problem or opportunity makes this change worth doing now?
2. Who is affected, in which workflow, and at what moment?
3. What business rules, permissions, policies, or invariants must hold?
4. What should become possible, easier, safer, or clearer after the change?
5. What is wrong, missing, inconsistent, or costly in the current state?
6. What is the smallest useful first slice, and what is explicitly out of scope?
7. What edge cases, failure states, or unusual users could break the proposal?
8. Which capabilities are new, and which existing capabilities change behavior?
